# Slide Capture Pro

Slide Capture Pro is an AI-assisted study tool designed to help students automatically extract clean lecture slides and important board content from educational YouTube videos.

The app intelligently analyzes lecture videos frame-by-frame and captures screenshots only at meaningful moments — such as when a teacher is about to switch slides or temporarily moves away from the board, allowing unobstructed capture of notes and diagrams.

Built with the help of Google AI Studio, the project focuses on reducing manual note-taking effort and helping students quickly generate organized visual study material from online lectures.

---

## ✨ Features

- 🎥 Extracts frames directly from YouTube lecture links
- 🧠 Detects slide transition moments automatically
- 👨‍🏫 Teacher-presence detection to avoid blocked content
- 📸 Captures clean screenshots only when the board/screen is visible
- 📚 Helps students create visual footnotes and revision material
- ⚡ Reduces the need for manual screenshotting during lectures

## 🚧 Current Status

Although most core features were generated and structured with the help of Google AI Studio, some problems related to:
- detection logic

- overall app stability

are still unresolved.

## 📚 Use Cases

- Online coaching lectures
- Revision from recorded classes
- Creating quick visual notes
- Saving diagrams, formulas, and important explanations
- Studying from YouTube educational content efficiently

---

## 🎯 Goal

The goal of Slide Capture Pro is to make learning more efficient for students by automating one of the most repetitive tasks during online study sessions — manually taking screenshots of important slides and board work.
